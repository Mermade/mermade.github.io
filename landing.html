About Mermade Software

Founder Mike Ralphson has over 25 years experience in software development, ranging from Node.js, Javascript, C# and Java to many flavours of SQL.


He is the maintainer of the APIs.guru collection of OpenAPI definitions, as well as a member of the OAI Technical Steering Committee (TSC) and the co-chair of the W3C Schema.Org WebAPI Discovery Group.


His blog is located at Dev.To.

This site features photography by Claire Ralphson-Cook taken at Bletchley Park.
